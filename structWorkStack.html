Detailed Description

template<typename FRAME_TYPE, size_t STACK_SIZE = 0>
struct WorkStack< FRAME_TYPE, STACK_SIZE >

The WorkStack template struct represents a series of WorkFrames following a heirarchy of call depths. Currently, only a STACK_SIZE of zero is supported.
