Detailed Description

template<typename PROGRAM_SPEC>
struct AsyncProgram< PROGRAM_SPEC >::Instance

Instances wrap around their program scope's DeviceContext. These differ from a program's DeviceContext object in that they perform automatic deallocation as soon as they drop out of scope.
