template<typename OP_SET>
struct PromiseEnum< OP_SET >

The PromiseEnum template struct is simply the combination of a PromiseUnion and a discriminant that annotates the contained type.

A PromiseEnum is just a tagged version of a PromiseUnion, combining the union type with a discriminant that annotates the type of Promise contained by the union.
